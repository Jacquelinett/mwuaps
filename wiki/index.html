About My Wish Upon A Parallel Star
My Wish Upon A Parallel Star (MWUAPS) is a story-driven game heavily inspired by To The Moon in term of gameplay.

The plot is about a girl see a shooting star and made a wish that she could see how different her life could have been if certain things in her life was changed before jumping off a building, the god of the shooting star grant her wish, and allow her to meet with her parallel self. Together, she and her parallel explore their similar yet different pasts, seeing how things are different, and ultimately trying to convince the girl to choose to stay alive.
