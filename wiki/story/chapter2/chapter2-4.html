2.4 [Location: Mari’s Flashback and Shooting Star Express]
[Scene Description]: Mari has a series of flashbacks at the beginning of this part. Her flashbacks are jumbled static grey screens with black silhouettes speaking to her. In her last flashback, one of the silhouettes turns out to be Airi’s wearing a white lab coat, indicating that he is a doctor. Airi, Comic, and Mari are standing near the entrance when Mari is out of her flashbacks. The Shooting Star remains unchanged for this part, except for Astraeus, who changed from his regular trench coat outfit into wearing a Hawaiian shirt and beach trunk, sitting on a foldable chair in the balcony.
Mari has a flashback and headache and remembers getting bullied, the name Shinri, and Airi’s dad being a doctor. Airi confirms that her dad is indeed a doctor and suggests that they go to Applewood’s Hospital next. Comic suggest that Mari got a headache because she’s hungry offers to cook Airi and Mari food, but it won’t be ready until they are back from the next trip. Airi and Mari head to the hospital while waiting.

[Gameplay]: The player can now take Airi to sit on the bed, where the next set of cutscenes will happen. Optionally, they can also interact with Comic who’s actually preparing some gear instead of cooking, and ask what he's doing. Comic will reply that he’s preparing to “hunt down the ferocious beasts” and “harvest fierce-colored fruit” to cook with. Airi will roll her eyes and ask him what he’s really making, in which Comic will squint his eye socket and sheepishly respond “Orange chicken”. Airi can also go interact with Astraeus where she will ask him “What the heck are you doing” and if the player pursues the conversation essentially Astraeus will give life meaning advice while looking ridiculous.

[Gameplay]: The player can now interact with the headwear device from chapters 2-2 again and play a 3x3 sliding puzzle. Once Airi is done, she can go back and ask Mari to go with her. There’s no dialog/cutscene for picking up Mari, she will simply join Airi’s party once the player asks and they can then head to the next destination. A similar but shorter train leaving scene (compared to 2-2) will play when the player use the machine. Optionally she can ask Comic if the food is ready yet, in which Comic will tell her to go on her “little trip” first and he will have everything ready by the time she’s back.
