2.5 [Location: Applewood Hospital]
[Scene Description]:

I’m actually not 100% sure how I want the hospital layout to be like, so this might be subject to change in the future. Airi and Mari will arrive through the portal door in the lobby of the hospital, where there will be a reception desk to the right with a nurse on staff. The side of the wall has an elevator that can take them to 3 different floors (including the current one) and the basement. There will be a hallway on the right side that leads to many treatment rooms. The second floors include several doctor offices, a common lounge with toys (place for employees’ children and patients’ children to play), and another hallway to the right with treatment rooms, but none of it will be important to the story and exists purely for easter eggs and exploration. There will however be an employee-only office near the end of the hallway, where a cutscene will occur. On the 3rd floor there will also be many doctor offices, but only one of them, Dr. Takahashi’s office, will be important. The basement is a surgery wing, with a waiting area and a door to a hallway that leads to various surgery rooms. There will be a cutscene in the waiting area that spans into one of the surgery rooms that is initially inaccessible due to surgery in progress.

The staff’s lounge has a cabinet full of candies inside, some toys, and various office equipment you would expect in a typical hospital - a coffee machine, printer, TV, cabinet full of instant foods like cups of noodles. Dr. Takahashi’s office is also a typical office, except there’s an extra desk with various college entrance exam prep books. During the cutscene here, young Airi will be sitting at the desk, studying, while her dad works on the computer at his desk. The waiting area and the surgery room in the basement are typical rooms you can find in a hospital. During the cutscene there, young Airi and her dad will be waiting in the waiting room. A doctor will come out to inform them of the outcome of the surgery, and they follow the doctor to the surgery room, in which Airi’s mom is laying in bed resting, and a baby is wrapped in a blanket in a basket nearby.


Airi and Mari visit Applewood hospital in the past when Airi’s little brother was about to be born. Once again, Airi and Mari couldn’t find a Mari anywhere, but Mari could recall more details about her life. Like Airi, Mari’s parents also apparently worked at the hospital, so they both have spent a lot of their childhood weekends at the hospitals. When Airi and Mari enter the delivery room, Mari was able to recall that she had a sister, and they bond over their similar past. Mari tried to remember more about her sister, but the only thing she couldn’t remember was herself crying. Afterward, Airi and Mari return to the Shooting Star Express.
