[Scene Description]:

Similar to previous scenes, I’m still not 100% sure on how I want the school layout to be, other than it’s American, maybe looping around to save on backtracking. There are a few important locations that I will describe, as it is important for the scenes, but where these locations go (and really the order in which they connect) is flexible. The important locations are locker rooms, field, history class, music class, library, and the school entrance.

The history class is a typical classroom setting, although, unlike the elementary school, this time it is more American inspired than Japanese inspired. The teacher desks are actually placed toward the back, behind the students. There is a whiteboard to the front, with a projector screen and a podium for when the teachers want to speak. The classrooms are decorated with motivational posters and various history props, such as German helmets and steam train sets. Young Airi and Dara will be sitting next to each other in the class. There will also be a girl named Lara who is also in the class.

The locker rooms are gendered, with a gym teacher’s office in the middle between them. Both locker rooms and the teacher’s office are connected to a typical track + grass field behind, which is fenced. The locker rooms are also typical American locker rooms, open changing (although there will be a bathroom connected on the side if someone chooses to not change in front of other people). The field size is medium size, probably the size of a professional basketball court, intended to be multipurpose fields that you can play most sports on despite the size and provided the equipment is set up. The track on the outside of the fields is rubberized, similar to a typical track field.

The library will have two rows of computers near the back, two rows of big/group study table to the front, a row of bench tables to the left along with the reception desk, and shelves of books to the right.

The music class is very similar in layout to the old elementary school music class - with a piano near the center and table/chair set up like a stadium/rounded fashion.

The front of the school, in a typically American fashion, will actually be a drop-off/pick-up road that leads to the parking lots. Young Airi and Dara will be waiting at the entrance, talking to each other. During the cutscene, Airi’s parents will arrive here in a car, and young Airi will enter the car while Airi and Mari will be climbing ON TOP of the car and sit there as the car drives away.


Airi and Mari explore Applewood’s high school, where they visited: 1. The gym - Mari remembers being bullied there and didn’t want to go in. 2. The history class where Mari remembers her best friend Lara and has a conversation with Airi about social anxiety. 3. Piano class where Mari remember a tune and played it on piano. 4. The library where Mari remembers spending most of her lunchtime there. Once all the locations was visited, the school bell rings, indicating classes are over and Airi and Mari head to the entrance to see if they can find young Mari in the crowd. Unfortunately, young Mari was nowhere to be seen. Instead, they decided to climb on top of Airi’s parents’ car when they arrived to pick young Airi up, thinking it would be a fun change of pace.
