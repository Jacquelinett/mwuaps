[Scene Description]: Similar to the hospital, I’m not solid on where things should be, there are only a few things that I specifically want in this part to play out in a cutscene, but otherwise things are left to Chloe to decide the layout. Ideally, I would like the zoo to loop around so that the player can explore from one end to the next and then loop back to the beginning to exit without needing to backtrack. The main attractions for the cutscenes would be enclosures of lions, elephants, and giraffes. The entrance in my imagination also has a water fountain with a wooden bench in front of it and an ice cream shop stand to the right, both of which will also be used in a cutscene. Young Airi and a young boy will be in most of the cutscene, going on a date while watching animals.
Airi and Mari arrive at the zoo, where Mari realizes Airi went on a date here when she was younger. Mari suggested that they search separately so as to not intrude on Airi’s more intimate life details. Airi observes her younger self’s date, questioning why she even liked the boy she dated in the first place. When they are done searching, Airi reveals to Mari that the boy she was seeing here actually dumped her the week after so this actually brings back a sore spot for her. Mari forces Airi to enjoy herself by dragging her across the zoo and experiencing going to the zoo as is without any worries/concern about figuring out who she is.
