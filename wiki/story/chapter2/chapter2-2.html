2.2 [Location: The Shooting Star Express]
[Scene Description]: The Shooting Star Express is also the same in this scene as in Chapter 1.9. Airi will be interacting with the strange headwear device and machine connected to the wall in the reception room. The device allows Airi to envision a memory from her past and let her navigate The Shooting Star Express there.
Comic explains to Airi how she can go about helping Mari. Since Astraeus told Airi that Mari and her are connected and Airi doesn’t recall ever meeting Mari, Airi thinks they might have gone to elementary together and decides to go there.

  • [Comic]: “Airi! Here you are. The preparation is...”

  • [Airi]: “I see”

I also have a plan for the player to be able to explore The Shooting Star Express again and find Astraeus, where they can press Astraeus for more info on who he is, but this will be optional. Mari will be on the bunk bed (same location as the previous chapter).

[Gameplay]: The player will be presented with a 2x2 very simple sliding puzzle minigame meant as a tutorial. To proceed they just need to solve it, in which the sliding puzzle will result in a picture of an elementary school. This represents the memory that Airi is thinking about.

[Scene]: The head device plays some short tunes, indicating that the destination is acquired.

  • [Comic]: “Good job Airi, I can see on the system that you were able to input a memory. Next up you just need to select the memory on the navigation device and we will take you there.

[Gameplay]: As indicated, the player needs to interact with the machine attached to the wall, where basically a selection UI will show up, listing all the destinations that they can visit. Once a memory is unlocked it will *always* available here, so should the player want to replay a section in the future, they can. Since there’s only one memory unlocked right now, that’s where we will go.
