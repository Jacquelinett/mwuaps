2.11 [Location: Mari’s Final Flashback/Airi Parent’s House and Aboard the Shooting Star Express]
[Scene Description]:

Unlike previous flashbacks, Mari’s flashback no longer has any jumbled static background (if the artist can make it we will try to have it gradually less static per flashback instead of having then not had static). Mari will be inside the living room. To the right of the living room is a hallway, with a stair, and further to the right would be the study room. South of the hallway is the dining room to the left and the kitchen to the right. Next to the stair are two rooms in a corner, one is the storage closet and the other is a bathroom. All the way south is the entrance to the house. There is a wall phone in the hallway.

The living room has a fireplace on the left wall, 2 sets of sofas making up the top and right corner, a coffee table in the center, and a TV to the south of the coffee table. The north wall has a big shelf with china decoration (and really whatever the artist wants :P).

There are two desks in the study room, both have a PC on them (I intend for it to mean one desk for Mari and the other desk for her dad) with various books/typical desktop stuff you usually see. There are also bookcases, shelves with decorations, decorations in general, and a piano at the center of the room.

The dining room and kitchen are very typical settings. I don't think I need to describe them. Expect a big family-style table you can eat at, a shelf/cabinet for silverware/plate/bowl, stoves, sink, etc.

The gameplay happens only inside of the study room, but the player can explore the surroundings to give proof that this is in fact Airi’s house since the player will be exploring the same location in the next part.

The Shooting Star Express remains the same in this part, with Astraeus in the balcony (although I might add rooms to the Shooting Star Express in the future just to give variety to where Astraeus can be) and Comic in the engine room.


Mari has a flashback about being inside a house, where it is revealed that it is Airi’s dad’s house, and Mari has been talking to Airi’s dad and referring to him also as her dad. Mari woke up with some suspicion and asked Airi to take her to another memory involving her parent’s house. While Airi sets it up, Mari has a conversation with Astraeus and he decides to join them for the next trip.

[Gameplay]: Mari wakes up on the sofa in the living room, looking confused. A moment after, the phone rings, and Mari says she probably should pick it up. The player then takes control of Mari and has to pick up the phone, in which the following conversation ensues:

  • [Mari]: “Hello”
  • [Dad]: “Mari? Your mom and I will be home in two hours. Have you done your homework yet?”
  • [Mari]: “No dad. I just woke up from a nap”
  • [Dad]: “Well please do your homework, and once you are done with that please practice the piano and do your practice SAT test”
  • [Mari]: “I know dad, you don’t have to keep reminding me”
  • [Dad]: “I will check your work once you are done. We will also drop by a restaurant for take-out on the way back, is there anything you want?”
  • [Mari]: “The usual is fine, dad”
  • [Dad]: “Sounds good. See you soon”
  • [Mari]: “See you soon dad”
  • [Mari put the phone down] and says out loud: “I should get started”

[Gameplay]: As the dialog suggests, to proceed, the player needs to 1. Have Mari do her homework (interact with Mari’s desk in the study hall), 2. Have Mari do her practice test (interact with her desk a second time), 3. Have Mari practice the piano (interact with the piano). There will be a short fade to black then back between each action, indicating some time has passed. Once Mari finishes all 3 actions, door unlocking noises will be heard, and Mari will stop whatever she was doing and run toward the hallway, where the front door will open and… Airi’s mom and dad walk in.

Once the player finishes the puzzle, Astraeus and Mari enter the reception room.
