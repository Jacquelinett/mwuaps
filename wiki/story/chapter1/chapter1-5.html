1.5 [Location: BigCorp Inc - 3rd Floor/Cafeteria, Cherrygrove - Barricade St]
[Scene Description]: Airi is at the Cafeteria on the 3rd floor, which has long counters with food displayed in cases on the right of the elevator, vending machine on the left. There are many tables in the room. Once Airi leave, the street she will be on will be the same as the previous scene, minus minor changes since people don’t stay in the same spot all day.
Airi has lunch with Dara and shares with her about various strange things that happened to her. Dara thinks Airi is probably just seeing things because she’s underslept from staying up late and stressing out over her presentation and suggests that she take the rest of the day off. Airi initially refuses, but after her food glitches and Dara doesn’t seem to notice it at all, Airi agrees to head home.

[Scene] Airi enters the Cafeteria and Dara waves at her from a table. Airi waves back to her and remarks that she should get some food then go sit with Dara.

[Gameplay] The player can control the player to buy some food from the counter as well as use the vending machine to get whatever they want. What the player buys won’t matter storywise. Once the player got Airi some food, they will need to go sit down with Dara.

[Scene]: Dara will make some remark about the kind of food the player got for Airi (as an example: “Chocolate? Your day were that bad huh?”), and Airi will responses “Yeah it’s been a crazy day”. To make it simple, we assume the player picks a salad for Airi.
