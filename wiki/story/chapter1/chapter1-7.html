1.7 [Location: Airi’s Dream, then Airi’s apartment, then Cherrygrove City - 30th Street]
[Scene Description]: In Airi’s dream, she and the blonde man is on a date at a beautiful skyline against a lake. There is a coffee stand, bathroom, and bench nearby them. When Airi wakes up, her apartment will still be the same, her cat will be sleeping next to her couch. Later on when Airi leaves her apartment, 30th street will be the same street, but barely any people.
Airi has a dream where she’s the brunette girl going on a date with the man from earlier. In the dream, Airi knows that the man is named Mark and the brunette girl is named Mari. Mark proposes to Mari in the dream. When Airi wakes up, it’s about 1 am. Hungry, she check the fridge but there wasn’t anything she wanted and leave for the subway to head one town over for an open takeout restaurant.

[Scene]: Airi and the man walk together, and Airi will remarks that “Wow, the skyline is so beautiful, Mark”. The man, who will be referred to as Mark from now on, smiles at Airi and hugs her. Mark tells Airi “It’s cold, do you want some coffee?” in which Airi will nod. Mark will tell Airi to “Pick your poison”

[Gameplay]: The player can now control Airi again, and Mark will follow Airi wherever she walks. To proceed, the player will need to interact with the coffee shop, giving them some coffee flavor choices. It doesn’t matter which they pick, although whatever choice they pick will be remembered for later. Mark will then ask Airi to sit down at the nearby bench, and the player will have to interact with the bench.

[Scene]: Once Airi sits down, Mark will sit down next to her. He turns around to look at the skyline, and remarks that it’s a beautiful night. Airi agrees with him, then he turns around, get off the bench, and on to his knee, he then grabs a box from his pocket, and tell Airi:
  • [Mark]: “But it isn’t as beautiful as you. Mari, I can’t imagine growing old without you, will you marry me?”
  • [Airi blushes]: “I... I would be happy to”
  • Mark put the ring onto Airi’s finger, then stand up and kiss her
  • [Airi]: Mark, this is embarrassing but can I go to the bathroom?
  • [Mark]: “Yeah of course”
  • Airi runs to the bathroom. Once inside, she squeals, flustered from excitement. She then walks toward the mirror to wash her hand, where the player will see that it’s not Airi in the mirror, but the brunette from the vision Airi had. When Airi notices the mirror, she wakes up from her dream.

[Scene]: Airi jolts awake as her TV is showing the local news discussing the suicide of an employee at Big Inc Corp. Airi, half asleep, turns the TV off. She realizes it was the news, remark “Oh shit” and turn the TV back on. Oddly enough, the news is no longer there, and instead, she is greeted with some cooking channels. Airi frantically change the channels, but couldn’t find the news again. Defeated, she turns it off and looks at her phone, remarks that it’s “Oh shit it’s 1 am. Fuck. I need to feed my cat and get some food.”

[Gameplay]: As expected, to proceed, the player will need to interact with the cat food bowl, then check the fridge. Airi will notice that there’s no food she wants to eat and check her phone for a food place that’s still open. Airi finds a place one subway station away, and the player will need to leave the apartment and walk out in the street to the 30th Street subway station.
