1.2 [Location: Airi's Apartment - Living Room]
[Scene Description]: Airi descends the stair into the living room. Her cat, Hunter, is sleeping at his bed at the bottom of the stair to the left. There is a fridge to the right, and next to the fridge is an expresso machine on top of a counter, and a stove with a pan on top. Near the center, there is a table with a folder on it, and next to the table there is a backpack. To the left of the room is the exit.
Airi feeds her cat, prepares and eats breakfast, then leaves for work.

[Scene] Hunter gets up from his bed and make a beeline toward Airi as she walks down the stair. He affectionately rubs his head against her leg, and Airi reciprocates it by leaning down to pet him.

  • [Airi]: “Good morning Hunter”
  • [Hunter]: “Meooowwwww”

[Scene] Airi walks over to the fridge and grabs a box of cat food and pours it into Hunter’s food bowl nearby then puts the box back.

  • [Airi]: “Hmm, I should make some breakfast and coffee”

[Gameplay]: Since this is a story-focused game, the player’s second task is to play a cooking minigame to help Airi prepares breakfast. To proceed, the player will need to:

  • Interact with the fridge, where Airi will get [Egg] and [Ham].
  • Interact with the expresso machine to turn it on.
  • Interact with the pan on the stove to initiate a cooking minigame, where the player will select and cook [Egg] and [Ham] together.

[Scene]: Airi, in a comical video game fashion, will open her mouth wide and slide the content of the pan into her mouth and gobble it up in one go. She will also down her coffee in one go. She then stretches her arm, remarking “Okay gotta go now”, grab her backpack next to the table and leave, only to come back after 3 seconds to grab the folder from the table before leaving again.
