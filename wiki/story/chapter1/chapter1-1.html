1.1 [Location: Airi's Apartment - Bedroom]
[Scene Description]: Airi is in her bedroom, on the 2nd floor. There is a bathroom to the left, and a door and stair that lead to the living room to the right. Airi is in her pajama, asleep in her bed. There is a pile of clothes on the floor, along with her purses, shoes, and socks. There are makeups smeared on Airi's face and pillow, and an alarm clock on the side of the bed.
Airi is woken up by her mom and get ready for work.
  • The alarm clock ring, making Airi groan as she tosses around her bed to slam the clock to turn it off.
  • Airi yawns and goes back to sleep, only to be disturbed by a phone call.
  • [Airi groans]: "Ugh, who would call this early in the morning--"
  • Airi grabs her phone and looks, then accepts the call.
  • [Airi]: "Mom!"
  • [Airi yawn]: "Good morning, a bit early to call isn't it?"
  • [Mom]: "Huh? What are you talking about? *You* asked me to call you and wake you up because you have an important presentation today, remember?"
  • [Airi rolls her eyes]: "Oh... right. Shit. Thanks, mom."
  • [Mom]: "Good luck, let me know how it goes"
  • Airi puts her phone down, sighs, and gets off the bed.
  • [Airi]: "Okay, let's get started with my day"

[Gameplay]: As an introduction to the game, the player’s first task is to help Airi get started with her day.

To proceed, the player will need to:

  • Interact with the sink in the bathroom. Airi will remark that she will get started with her day and the screen will fade to black then back with Airi in makeup.
  • Interact with the closet, Airi will receive “Work Uniform, and the player will be prompted to open their inventory and “use” the outfit. Airi will change her clothes
  • Leave the room
