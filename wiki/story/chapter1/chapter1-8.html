1.8 [Location: Subway, then Spirit Station]
[Scene Description]: Airi gets on an empty subway. Due to the glitches, she will arrive at the Spirit Station, which is also empty and look abandoned and broken down. There will be a stair to the right of the station where a skeleton will be running down during one of the scenes, and there will be 2 boards, one for schedule on the left and another one for the network map on right.
Airi goes on the subway, intending on getting off at Cathedral St station to go get some food. However, the subway glitches and drop Airi off at some abandoned station with a train instead. Airi meets Comic, a talking skeleton and train conductor for the Shooting Star Express. Comic explains to Airi that she’s here because she made a wish upon a shooting star. Comic convince Airi to go with him to see his boss, and if Airi still wants to head home after, his boss will take her home. Airi reluctantly agrees and gets on the train.

[Scene]: Airi gets on the subway, sits on one of the benches, and looks at her phone. A moment after, the PA announce that the subway is arriving at the Cathedral Street Station. Suddenly, the whole subway glitch and the PA announce “Now Arriving At !@#$%^”. Airi looks up for her phone, confused as to what had just happened as the subway slow down and eventually stop. Airi nervously poke her head out of the opened door and slowly walk outside.

[Gameplay]: The player will take Airi to explore the abandoned station, interacting with a schedule board - Airi will remark that she does not understand how this schedule works, and a map of the subway station - Airi does not recognize the system. Once done, a train horn will be heard in the distance, and a train will slowly come to a stop near the station. Airi will need to walk and interact with it, where she will remark that she has never seen a train like it before.
