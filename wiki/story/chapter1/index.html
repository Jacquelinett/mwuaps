1.1 [Location: Airi's Apartment - Bedroom]
[Scene Description]: Airi is in her bedroom, on the 2nd floor. There is a bathroom to the left, and a door and stair that lead to the living room to the right. Airi is in her pajama, asleep in her bed. There is a pile of clothes on the floor, along with her purses, shoes, and socks. There are makeups smeared on Airi's face and pillow, and an alarm clock on the side of the bed.
Airi is woken up by her mom and get ready for work.
  • The alarm clock ring, making Airi groan as she tosses around her bed to slam the clock to turn it off.
  • Airi yawns and goes back to sleep, only to be disturbed by a phone call.
  • [Airi groans]: "Ugh, who would call this early in the morning--"
  • Airi grabs her phone and looks, then accepts the call.
  • [Airi]: "Mom!"
  • [Airi yawn]: "Good morning, a bit early to call isn't it?"
  • [Mom]: "Huh? What are you talking about? *You* asked me to call you and wake you up because you have an important presentation today, remember?"
  • [Airi rolls her eyes]: "Oh... right. Shit. Thanks, mom."
  • [Mom]: "Good luck, let me know how it goes"
  • Airi puts her phone down, sighs, and gets off the bed.
  • [Airi]: "Okay, let's get started with my day"

[Gameplay]: As an introduction to the game, the player’s first task is to help Airi get started with her day.

To proceed, the player will need to:

  • Interact with the sink in the bathroom. Airi will remark that she will get started with her day and the screen will fade to black then back with Airi in makeup.
  • Interact with the closet, Airi will receive “Work Uniform, and the player will be prompted to open their inventory and “use” the outfit. Airi will change her clothes
  • Leave the room

1.2 [Location: Airi's Apartment - Living Room]
[Scene Description]: Airi descend the stair into the living room. Her cat, Hunter, is sleeping at his bed at the bottom of the stair to the left. There is a fridge to the right, and next to the fridge is an expresso machine on top of a counter, and a stove with a pan on top. Near the center there is a table with a folder on it, and next to the table there is a backpack. To the left of the room is the exit.
Airi feeds her cat, prepares and eats breakfast, then leaves for work.

[Scene] Hunter get up from his bed and made a beeline toward Airi as she walk down the stair. He affectionately rub his head against her leg, and Airi reciprocate it by leaning down to pet him.

  • [Airi]: “Good morning Hunter”
  • [Hunter]: “Meooowwwww”

[Scene] Airi walk over to the fridge and grab the box of cat food and pour it into Hunter’s food bowl nearby then put the box back.

  • [Airi]: “Hmm, I should make some breakfast and coffee”

[Gameplay]: Since this is a story focused game, the player’s second task is to play a cooking minigame to help Airi prepare breakfast. To proceed, the player will need to:

  • Interact with the fridge, where Airi will get [Egg] and [Ham].
  • Interact with the expresso machine to turn it on.
  • Interact with the pan on the stove to initiate a cooking minigame, where the player will select and cook [Egg] and [Ham] together.

[Scene]: Airi, in a comical video game fashion, will open her mouth wide and slide the content of the pan into her mouth and gobble it up in one go. She will also down her coffee in one go. She then stretch her arm, remarking “Okay gotta go now”, grab her backpack next to the table and leave, only to quickly to come back after 3 second to grab the folder from the table before leaving again.
