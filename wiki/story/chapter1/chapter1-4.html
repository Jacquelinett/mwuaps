1.4 [Location: Cherrygrove - Barricade St, BigCorp Inc - Lobby and 35th Floor]
[Scene Description]: Airi arrives at the station, then walks to her work, which is a 51-story skyscraper all the way to the left. On the way, Airi will pass a lake. On the first floor of the skyscraper is the lobby, where there is a reception desk with 2 employees. To the right are the entrance, a security guard, and a security system that prevents anyone without a badge from entering. To the left is an elevator. On the 35th floor, to the left of the elevator is a row of cubicles with monitors and various personal belonging. The walls are glasses that let the employee look out and see a beautiful skyline and a river. There are 3 doors inside, one all the way to the left, which lead to Airi’s boss’s room, and 2 down at the bottom, which leads to two different conference room. To the right of the elevator is the bathroom then the pantry. There is a potted plant on the left of the elevator.
Airi arrives at work and checks her surroundings but no sign of police or commotion. She goes on with her days - talking to her coworker Dara, giving her presentation, and doing her job. Strange glitches happen to her throughout the day, such as her empty coffee cup suddenly becoming full again, or her presentation slides suddenly changing and her laptop crashing.

[Gameplay]: Airi exit the train station, and the player can control her again. Airi will remark that she should get to work, and the player will have to do so.

[Scene] Once Airi is at her work building, she scans around for any cops or crime scenes, but finds nothing. Airi remarks that she must have imagined it.

[Gameplay]: Player can now enter the building, take the elevator up to the 35th floor.

[Scene]: Airi exit the elevator, and from the pantry, Dara walks out, holding 2 cups of coffee.

  • [Dara smug]: “Airi! Saw any dead body on the way?”
  • [Airi sigh and glares at Dara]: “Dara. Not funny”.
  • [Dara]: “Hehe, sorry. Are you ready for your earning report?”.
  • [Airi]: “Probably. Probably not. I don’t know. At this point, I just want to get it over and done with”
  • [Dara gives Airi a cup of coffee]: “Well, good luck girl. Here’s your coffee. Let me know how it goes, see you at lunch yeah?”
  • [Airi]: “Yup. See you at lunch”.
  • Dara press the elevator and the door open, she enters, waves to Airi as the door closes. The elevator descends and Airi walks to the left. The camera does not follow Airi however, and once Airi is off-screen the potted plant on the left of the elevator glitches, disappears, and reappears on the right side. The scene fades to black, then to Airi at her desk.
  • Airi puts her cup of coffee down, takes open her backpack, gets her laptop, and hooks it to the monitor. She remarks that she still has some time before her meeting and should take some time to review it.

[Gameplay]: The player can now explore Airi’s office, but more importantly, they can look at the Airi’s screen and open the summary file, containing bullet points information about quarterly earnings reports, customer satisfaction surveys, etc. It is important that the player review this because the information in this file will be used in an Ace Attorney style minigame coming up next.

  • Once the player is ready, they can walk into a presentation room at the bottom where there are already a few businesspeople sitting inside.
  • The minigame will start, where Airi will be showing a Powerpoint presentation while giving a talk. Occasionally, Airi will be interrupted by a listener, and they will ask her questions. The player will have to answer them with the correct response (from the summary the player read earlier).
  • The player will also have the option to check the summary file during the minigame in case they forget something.
  • Every time Airi answers a question right, she gains more confidence, and after 4 correct answers, Airi will continue with her presentation as the screen fade to black then back to Airi saying “And that concludes my reports” and everyone in the room applauds.

[Scene] One by one, the businesspeople walk up to Airi, shake her hand, tell her variation of “Good job” and exit the room. Once everyone left, Airi let out a sigh and slumps her chair, looking at the ground. Just then, the screen glitch, changing her presentation on her projector to a slide about “Applied Machine Learning Usecase - Customer Satisfaction”. Airi notices it and is visibly confused. She remarks “What the heck”. Before she could look into the presentation, her computer display the blue screen of death and restart itself. Airi shrugs it off, remarking “Must be a bug. Oh well, I gotta get back to work”.

[Gameplay]: The player can now walk around again, but once they sit down at Airi’s desk, their movement will be switched to moving in a rolling chair. While they are sitting on the chair, the movement will be limited, but there will be 3 actions they can only do while sitting in the chair. The 3 actions are:

  • Interact with a stack of paperwork
    • Airi picks up a pen from a cup on her desk, and as she looks away to fill out some paperwork, her cup glitches and there’s an identical pen appearing in the cup.
    • When Airi finishes with her paperwork and puts her pen back, she remarks “Have there always been two pens here?”.
  • Interact with her coffee
    • Airi gulps her coffee, finishes it, then puts the cup down. She turns to her laptop to work. Her cup glitches as she looks away. A moment after, Airi stops and unconsciously reaches for her supposedly empty cup and drinks from it.
    • Airi puts the cup down keeps working for a bit before realizing what just happened. She remarks “Wait, didn’t I finish the coffee earlier?”.
  • Interact with Airi’s laptop
    • As Airi works, a man walks toward her cubicle and knock-on the cubicle’s wall. Airi look at him.
    • [Man]: “Airi, bossman said these documents need to be finished ASAP today”.
    • [Airi]: “Are you for real?”.
    • [Man put a stack of documents on Airi’s desk]: “Sorry, I’m just the messenger”.
    • [Airi]: “I know. I will get it done by lunch”.
    • Airi turns around to work on her laptop. The stack of documents on her desk glitches and disappear. A moment after the same man walk toward her cubicle and knock again
    • [Man]: “Airi, bossman said these documents need to be finished ASAP today”.
    • [Airi]: “MORE?”
    • [Man raise his eyebrow]: “What do you mean? It has been a while since we asked you to help with the documents…”
    • [Airi look at where the old stack of documents used to be]: “Didn’t you just give me... Whaaat?“
    • [Man put the stack of documents on Airi desk]: “This is the first time I talked to you today Airi. I gotta go, but please try to get it done before today’s end”.
    • [Airi look confused]: “Okay, I will get it done by lunch”.
    • Screen fade to black and back to Airi remarking “Whew I finished the documents”
  • Once all 3 events are done, Airi gets a text from Dara, telling her she’s in the Cafeteria now. And the player can control Airi to head down to the 3rd floor - the Cafeteria.
