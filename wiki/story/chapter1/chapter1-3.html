1.3 [Location: Cherrygrove City - 30th Street, then subway]
[Scene Description]: Airi exits her apartment and is outside on a street full of townhouses. The street has a typical city street scenery, based on New York City. At the end of the street to the left is the subway station. The subway Airi will be on is crowded, but has enough room for walking and sitting.
Airi heads to work via the subway. On the way, she sees a shooting star and wishes that her life is more than just a boring office job. Just then, the TV on the subway glitches and broadcasts breaking news of someone potentially suicide by jumping off the same building that Airi works at. No one around here seems to notice. Her co-worker Dara who was already at work does not see anything going on at the building.

[Gameplay]: The player now has a section of the city open up, where they are free to explore around. For the most part, there won’t be any important things the player needs to do, but there will be minigames and easter eggs sprinkled around for the player to enjoy. To proceed, the player will need to:

  • Enter the subway station.
  • Purchase a ticket to Barricade Street, and Airi will automatically wait and enter the next arrived subway.

[Scene] Airi enters a subway. The subway starts moving. After a few seconds, Airi hears a ding from her phone and checks.

  • She got a text from her mom asking if she left for work yet. As she texts her mom back, the subway exits the tunnel, and the scene zooms out to see a full cinematic city view as the game’s title drops.
  • Airi takes a moment to enjoy the view without finishing her text. Just then a shooting star shoots across the sky. Airi noticed it and jokingly wished “Man it’s a Monday I don’t want to work. I wish my life is more exciting than just an office job”.
  • Airi finishes responding to her mom. Her mom responds back “Good luck with your meeting”, and Airi smile, then put her phone away and enjoy the view some more.
  • Just then, the TV inside the subway suddenly glitches, and a piece of breaking news started broadcasting:
    • News Reporter: “Breaking news today reporting to you live! This morning outside the headquarters of BigCorp Inc. remains of someone who appeared to have fallen off the fifty-one-story building, Police have yet to identify the victim but they seem to be leaning more into the motive of suicide as there seemed to be no signs of foul play--”.
  • Airi exclaims out loud “Wait, that’s where I work”. She quickly texts her best friend and coworker - Dara:
    • [Airi]: “Dara, are you at work? Did you see the breaking news?”.
    • [Dara]: “What news? The WcDonald opening down the street? And yeah I’m at work”
    • [Airi]: “No. Not that. I just saw on the news that someone died falling off our building. Can we even go to work today?”.
    • [Dara]: “Well I only got here about 10 minutes ago, but I don’t see anything like that. Everyone seems to be working as normal. But I will go check”.
  • Airi looks up from her phone and sees that no one else seems to be paying any attention to the breaking news. As she looks around, the TV glitches and broadcasts a cooking show. Airi’s phone ring.
    • [Dara]: “Nope, I looked, no cops, no dead body. Are you okay Airi?”.
  • Airi quickly glances around the subway one more time - no one else seems concerned. Airi text Dara back.
    • [Airi]: “I… might have imagined it then. Sorry, I’m almost there, see you in a bit”.
  • The PA announce that the subway is arriving at Barricade Street. The subway then slows to a stop, and Airi exits.
